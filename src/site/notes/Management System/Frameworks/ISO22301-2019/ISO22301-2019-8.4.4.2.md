# Description

Collectively, the business continuity plans shall contain: a) details of the actions that the teams will take in order to: 1) continue or recover prioritized activities within predetermined time frames; 2) monitor the impact of the disruption and the organizationï¿½s response to it; b) reference to the pre-defined threshold(s) and process for activating the response; c) procedures to enable the delivery of products and services at agreed capacity; d) details to manage the immediate consequences of a disruption giving due regard to: 1) the welfare of individuals; 2) the prevention of further loss or unavailability of prioritized activities; 3) the impact on the environment.
