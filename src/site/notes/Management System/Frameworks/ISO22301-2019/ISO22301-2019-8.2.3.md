# Description

The organization shall implement and maintain a risk assessment process. The organization shall: a) identify the risks of disruption to the organizationï¿½s prioritized activities and to their required resources; b) analyse and evaluate the identified risks; c) determine which risks require treatment. NOTE The process for risk assessment is addressed in ISO 31000. NOTE Risks in this subclause relate to the disruption of business activities. Risks and opportunities related to the effectiveness of the management system are addressed in 6.1.
